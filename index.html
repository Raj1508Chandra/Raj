About Special Get-together & Felicitation Program

The office of Alumni Affairs is pleased to announce that IIIT Allahabad will host the Annual “Global Alumni Meet 2023 (GAM2023)” in a Mixed mode from 02 – 03 September’2023. We are delighted to invite you to the Alumni Get-together and Felicitation Program, on September 02, 2023 at IIIT Allahabad (for alumni in Prayagraj and nearby places) and Global Virtual Alumni Meet (GVAM), on September 03, 2023 (for alumni all over the Globe) in Online mode. These events are an opportunity for us to come together and celebrate the accomplishments of our alumni, as well as to foster closer ties within our alumni community. So, Get ready to relive those cherished moments, reconnect with old friends, and reignite the flames of nostalgia as we invite you to the most awaited event of the year: the Global Alumni Meet/Special Get-together and Felicitation Ceremony. Step back in time and immerse yourself in the warmth of familiar faces, echoing laughter, and stories that have woven the tapestry of your life's journey .

Brief Bio-Data

Vishwa Nath Pratap Singh, an alumnus of B.Tech (IT), 2004 batch, holds over 12 years of experience in the realm of Big Data Platform, JAVA development, Performance analysis, and Network security, along with related technologies. His impactful journey includes steering the batch processing team at Flipkart as its Lead. With a profound expertise in architecting and designing intricate Big Data Systems, Vishwa Nath has adeptly wielded prominent tools such as Hadoop, Hive, Tez, Elasticsearch, Vertica, and Aerospike. Noteworthy is his instrumental role in reimagining data ingestion flows, showcasing his innovative prowess. His proficiency isn't confined; it extends seamlessly to Azure data factory, Cosmos DB, PostgreSQL, and NoSQL, solidifying his prowess across diverse technological landscapes. Additionally, Vishwa Nath's domain extends to Amazon Cloud, where he has showcased adeptness with HTTP, ICAP, ARP, and SNMP protocols. Evident is his keen acumen in performance benchmarking and tuning, a craft he refines using both TCL and JAVA automation to yield optimal outcomes. Vishwa Nath has seamlessly embraced Agile and Scrum models, and he's even found affinity with the Kanban model. His dynamic approach knows no geographical boundaries, as he has enriched his journey through onsite work experiences, including notable contributions in Latvia.



Brief Bio-Data

Vineet is an alum of B.Tech (ECE), 2007 batch. Right after college, Vineet moved to the consulting field, where he worked with Grail Research (Part of UK-based Monitor group) as a consultant/analyst. He helped Fortune 500 companies with business and market entry strategies. Parallely, he pursued CFA (USA), and after clearing 2 levels, he moved to the field of finance. He worked with a US-based firm named Trefis as an Equity Research expert, where he authored analyses that were published on NASDAQ and NYSE listed blue-chip companies in Forbes, The Wall Street Journal, and Seeking Alpha. Vineet is now the Co-founder and Managing Partner of Venture Garage, an Investment Bank that helps startups scale to $100 million in valuation by raising capital for them. He manages a network of 2,500+ VC/PE/Angel investors across India, the US, Dubai, and Singapore. Some of his successful assignments include Happilo (snacking brand), which is a $200 million valuation company that raised $40 million+ from A91 Partners and Motilal Oswal; 91sqft (prop-tech company), valued at $100 million+, raised $11 million+ from Stellaris VP, Y-Combinator (US), and Betatron (Hong Kong); Wobot - funded by Sequoia Capital; Designx - funded by Accel Partners. Prominent angel investors like Celeb Rannvijay Singh, Nitish Mittersain (Promoter of Nazara Technologies), Rahul Dash (Promoter of the Unicorn startup Purplle), among many others, have also invested in Vineet’s startups.


Vineet Sagar,
